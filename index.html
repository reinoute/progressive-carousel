Progressive Carousel

  • Swipe an image or drag it with your mouse to cycle through the carousel
  • Resize your browser to see how images with different resolutions are being loaded
  • Click an image to open fullscreen and view the high resolution image
  • Use the keyboard (TAB-key) to use the next/previous buttons and toggle fullscreen mode. In fullscreen mode, press the arrow keys to cycle through images.
  • Disable JavaScript to see a list of thumbnails. Click on a thumbnail to go to the high resolution image.
